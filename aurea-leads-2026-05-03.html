Market Trends — Costa Rica Real Estate 2025–2026
📉
Buyer's Market / Price Correction
Median home prices dropped 41% (Jan 2024: $907K → Jan 2025: $533K). Buyers have leverage. Sellers need competitive positioning via professional marketing to stand out.
🌊
Coastal Markets Lead Recovery
Escazú, Santa Ana, and Nosara driving price appreciation in 2026 (+5–8% coastal). Vacation rental demand and tourism are key demand drivers.
🌐
Foreign Buyer Dominance
US buyers make up the majority of foreign demand. Political/economic news cycles in the US directly impact CR buyer sentiment — campaigns must be crisis-resilient.
⏱️
Longer Time on Market
Homes in the Central & South Pacific taking longer to sell. Sellers compete on price AND presentation. Professional photography, video, and staging content = higher conversions.
🏗️
New Development Projects Active
Las Catalinas announcing 28 new residences (starting $450K) in 2026. New projects need full go-to-market campaigns: branding, launch strategy, digital ads.
🛏️
Short-Term Rental / Eco Tourism
Dual-purpose properties (private + rental) growing. "Pura vida" wellness tourism and eco-investment hotspots expanding. Content marketing opportunity around lifestyle positioning.
