Market Overview

Costa Rica Tourism Market Boom

The Costa Rica tourism market is valued to increase by USD 2.23 billion between 2025 and 2030, growing at an 8.1% CAGR. International tourism dominates (USD 3.97B in 2024), driven by sustainability-focused experiential travel. Sun & beach remains the largest segment, but eco-luxury lodges and wellness retreats are the fastest-growing categories.

2026 Hotel Marketing Key Trends

Hotels increasingly prioritize direct booking strategy over OTA commissions, using metasearch optimization (Google Hotel Ads, Tripadvisor). Short-form video (TikTok, Instagram Reels) is now a genuine booking channel, not just inspiration. AI-powered personalization and first-party data ownership are critical given cookie deprecation. Luxury travelers increasingly research via AI assistants, making brand authority and structured content essential.

Step 2 — Email Cold Outreach

Subject Lines That Cut Through

Include 1-paragraph relevant insight. No attachments in first email. Use Calendly link for easy scheduling.
